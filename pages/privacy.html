Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

At Spiral Tool, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our free online image editing tools.

ðŸ”’ Privacy First Approach

Your images never leave your device. All image processing happens locally in your browser using advanced web technologies. We cannot see, access, or store any images you edit with our tools.

Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Images or Files: We do not upload, store, or have access to any images you process with our tools
  • Personal Content: We cannot see the content of your images or any editing operations you perform
  • File Names: We do not collect or store the names of files you upload
  • Editing History: We do not track or store what edits you make to your images

Information We May Collect

  • Website Usage Data: Basic analytics about how our website is used (page views, time spent, general location)
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, and screen resolution to optimize user experience
  • Feedback Information: When you voluntarily contact us, we collect your name, email, and message content

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve our website's performance and user experience
  • Understand which tools are most popular to guide future development
  • Respond to user feedback and support requests
  • Ensure our website functions properly across different devices and browsers

Data Processing and Storage

Local Processing

All image editing operations are performed locally in your web browser using JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas technology. This means:

  • Images are processed on your device's processor
  • No image data is transmitted to our servers
  • You maintain complete control over your files
  • Processing speed depends on your device's capabilities

No Server-Side Storage

We do not store any user-generated content on our servers. When you upload an image to our tools:

  • The image stays in your browser's temporary memory
  • Processing happens entirely on your device
  • Results are generated locally and can be downloaded immediately
  • No copies are made on our servers at any point

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

Website Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand website usage patterns. These services collect anonymized data about page views and user interactions.

Content Delivery Networks (CDN)

We use CDNs to deliver CSS frameworks and fonts faster. These services may log basic request information.

Email Services

When you contact us through our feedback form, your message is processed through email service providers to deliver it to us.

Cookies and Local Storage

Our website may use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your tool settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors use our website
  • Local Storage: To temporarily store tool preferences in your browser

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality but will not impact image processing capabilities.

Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your privacy:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission to our website is encrypted
  • No Data Retention: We don't store personal images or editing data
  • Secure Hosting: Our website is hosted on secure, reputable platforms
  • Regular Updates: We keep our software and security measures up to date

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your privacy:

  • Complete Control: You have full control over any images you process
  • No Account Required: Use our tools without creating accounts or providing personal information
  • Contact Us: Request information about any data we may have collected
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics through browser settings or ad blockers

Children's Privacy

Our services are designed to be safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect personal information or store images, our tools can be used safely by children. However, we recommend parental supervision for children under 13 when using any online services.

International Users

Our website can be accessed from anywhere in the world. Since all image processing happens locally on your device, your images never cross international borders through our service. Any minimal data we collect is processed in accordance with international privacy standards.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be highlighted on our website
  • Continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance

Transparency Statement

We believe in complete transparency about our privacy practices. Here's our commitment to you:

  • We will never sell your data to third parties
  • We will never add hidden tracking without notice
  • We will continue to prioritize local processing
  • We will maintain our no-account-required policy
  • We will always be honest about any data we collect

Privacy Policy Summary

Bottom Line: Spiral Tool is designed with privacy as the top priority. Your images are processed entirely on your device, we don't require accounts, and we collect minimal data only to improve our service. Your creative work stays private and secure.
